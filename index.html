The Why

Edward Von Va Bene, a traveller of space, knitter of mittens, master-petter of dogs, decided a spring morning in 1837 the world needed a place to be in peace. However, he loved drinking, so a combination of the two was required. Thus, to his dogs surprise, he stopped in the middle of a stroke, and whispered: "Va Bene, Va Bene Bar" Legend has it the dog burst out in a howl that can still be heard in the most silent of nights, a sip of alcohol, and a dash of belief.

The Secret

Weekend after weekend, month after month, people of all kinds came to explore the signature drinks of the infamous Holy Grail, the Atlantis, the Solitude, and The Shepherd - ingredients of which never to be revealed. Loud mouths, drunken bastards, and broken glass, forced Edward to close the bar. Torn by the immediate success he turned to the drawing board, trying to find a way to fulfil his dream of a perfect bar. He realised, it had to be secret with only one answer.
